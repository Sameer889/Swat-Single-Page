First and foremost is the Kalam Valley which is located at a distance of 99 km away from the Swat Valley. The fluvial river of Swat encircled the whole valley along with the richly green hills. The Kalam Valley capture millions of tourist to its lap. Some of the notable place to visit within Kalam is of Matiltan, Usho, Utror, and Gabral, each has its own significance. With the cool and pleasant weather with panoramic locations, Kalam makes a perfect combination for the tourist.

The best time to visit Kalam valley is from April to September and winters can be really harsh here. Furthermore, the valley the perfect to visit while exploring the beauties of Swat and tourist love to do fishing, hiking in its enchanting sceneries.
