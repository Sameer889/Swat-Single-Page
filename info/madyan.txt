One of the most popular hill stations of Swat is Madyan. The hill station is preferable for the tourist because of the Swat River and its surrounding. The whole area offers panoramic views of the valley along with a pleasant atmosphere. Tourist love to visit Madyan because of its delicious mouthwatering trout fish and amazing weather even in hot summer. The fluvial Swat River capture more of the tourist to this hill station. Several hotels serve their lavish services in Madyan.
